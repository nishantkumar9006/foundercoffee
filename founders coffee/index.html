About Us

Founders Coffee is locally owned and locally focused, offering handcrafted beverages and food every day from the Southwest valley and Henderson. Featuring a drivethru and friendly service, Founders Coffee is the perfect space for coffee, meet-ups, and co-working. Drinks - Honoring the rich history and discovery of one of the world's most popular beverages, Founders Coffee offers a selection of globally sourced blends including a Medium and Dark Roast Coffee as well as a Dark Roast Espresso. The blends showcase an array of flavors and aromas ranging from nutty and smooth to earthy and bold with cocoa afternotes. In addition to classic coffee beverages like the latte, pour-over, cold brew, americano and cappuccino, Founders' menu includes lattes, smoothies and teas.
